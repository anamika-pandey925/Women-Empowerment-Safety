## Slide 2: The Problem
- Rising concerns regarding women's safety in public and digital spaces.
- Lack of immediate, reliable SOS response networks.
- Information gap regarding legal rights, self-defense tactics, and financial independence.
- Fear of judgment preventing victims from reporting incidents.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (Suraksha)
- **Reactive Safety:** 1-Tap SOS, Guardian Network status, and rapid emergency alerts.
- **Proactive Empowerment:** Legal rights education, job schemes, and self-defense videos.
- **Community Support:** Anonymous reporting and supportive community forums.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features
1. **AI Safety Dashboard:** Live threat level monitoring and network connection.
2. **Anonymous Reporting:** Describe incidents securely without revealing identity.
3. **Culture & Education:** Access modules on Cyber Security and Legal Rights (Domestic Violence Act, POSH Act).
4. **Multilingual Support:** Reach wider audiences across India (English, Hindi).

---

## Slide 5: Technical Stack
- **Frontend (UI/UX):** React.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vite.
  - Highly responsive, accessible, and supports Dark/Light mode.
- **Backend (Server):** Node.js, Express.js.
  - RESTful APIs for secure data transmission.
- **Database (Storage):** 
  - **MySQL:** Securely handles user credentials and roles.
  - **Local Storage:** Manages offline-capable data like user settings and forum cache.

---

## Slide 6: Security & Architecture
- **Password Hashing:** `bcrypt` integration ensures credentials are completely unreadable in the database.
- **Client-Side Persistence:** `useLocalStorage` hooks ensure seamless session recovery.
- **Fast Rendering:** Radix UI primitives guarantee accessible, keyboard-navigable dialogs and menus.
